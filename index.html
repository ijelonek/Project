About Virtuoso Biblio

Virtuoso Books are a chain of bookstores dedicated to providing you with the most beautiful bookshelf. Our expert team of beautiful book experts have searched far and wide to fill our stores with Classic, Platinum and Modern Editions for you to peruse and purchase and embrace and enjoy. Virtuoso Bibilio are commited to finding and displaying all the most stunning, visually-appealing, aesthetically-pleasing tomes. Whether you are an avid book collector or looking for the most amazing gift for the book lover in your life - we are here to provide.

I'll Give You The Sun by Jandy Nelson

Jude and her twin Noah were incredibly close - until a tragedy drove them apart, and now they are barely speaking. Then Jude meets a cocky, broken, beautiful boy as well as a captivating new mentor, both of whom may just need her as much as she needs them. What the twins don't realize is that each of them has only half the story and if they can just find their way back to one another, they have a chance to remake their world...

Our Stores

First Virtuoso Biblio store opened in April 1966 in a small street in Central London. It soon became popular with local artist, professors and students who appreciated that they could find the rerest gems in literature in the small, family run and charming store. Since then Virtuoso Biblio have opened three more stores across London which enable all book lovers to visit us without having to travel to Central London.

Virtuoso Books Central was the first family-run store opened in 1966. In 1970 the store won the best London bookshop award.

Virtuoso Biblio Central
12-14 Chatham Street
WC1 4TY
London

Opening hours:
Mon - Thu: 9:00 - 20:00
Fri - Sun: 10:00 - 19:00

Virtuoso Biblio East is located on a vibrant Hackney high street. Our long opening hours mean that people who finish work later in the day can still visit us to pick up a lovely read for the evening.

Virtuoso Biblio East
67 Mare Street
E9 6PL
London

Opening hours:
Mon - Fri: 9:30 - 21:30
Sat -Sun: 11:00 - 20:00

Virtuso Biblio South was our first store opened south of the river which rapidly expanded over the years. The store now stocks unique books in various disciplines and attract many regular customers.

Virtuoso Biblio South
54 Peckham Rye
SE8 3RE
London

Opening hours:
Mon - Sat: 10:00 - 19:30
Sun: 11:00 - 18:00

Virtuoso Biblio North is our youngest store which has been opened since 2004. It has became very popular with the residents of Finchley. The store has receive the best local bookshop award in 2008 and has also featured in Bookshop Life documentary on Channel 4.

Virtuoso Biblio North
113 Finchley Avenue

London
